+ About Us +

Welcome! We are SeeO2free, where innovation meets environmental stewardship. At SeeO2free, we are dedicated to revolutionizing the fight against vehicle carbon emissions. Our cutting edge technologies and solutions are designed to significantly reduce the impact of transportation on air quality, paving the way for a cleaner, healthier planet. Through our relentless commitment to sustainability, we aim to inspire individuals, communities, and industries to embrace eco friendly alternatives and join us in shaping a greener future for generations to come. Join us in our mission to save our air, one vehicle at a time.

Started — Founded in 2018 with the vision of combating vehicle carbon emissions.

Founders — Driven by a team of passionate environmentalists and technologists.

Country — Headquartered in Sri Lanka, Colombo 03, but with a global mission.

Mission — Dedicated to reducing air pollution caused by vehicle emissions worldwide.

Innovation — Pioneering groundbreaking technologies to tackle carbon emissions effectively.

Impact — Striving to create a cleaner and healthier environment for future generations.

Collaboration — Partnering with governments, industries, and communities to drive change.

Sustainability — Committed to promoting sustainable transportation solutions.

Community — Engaging with stakeholders to raise awareness and foster collective action.

Transparency — Operating with transparency and integrity in all our endeavors.
